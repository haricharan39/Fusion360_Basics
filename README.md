# Fusion360\_Basics



Fusion 360 practice designs and CAD models



## Introduction



This repository contains various mechanical designs and 3D models created using Autodesk Fusion 360 to improve practical understanding of CAD modeling, mechanical design, and product development. The designs were developed as part of learning and experimentation in mechanical engineering, with a growing interest in robotics, automation, and intelligent mechanical systems.

The work in this repository focuses on developing engineering visualization skills, assembly design techniques, and accurate 3D modeling practices used in real-world mechanical and automation applications.



## Textbook and Learning Resources Followed



The designs and modeling concepts were developed by referring to standard mechanical engineering and CAD learning resources, including:

* Engineering Drawing and Design concepts
* Machine Drawing principles
* Fusion 360 tutorials and documentation
* CAD modeling practice exercises
* Mechanical design reference materials

## Key Learnings



This work provided practical experience in CAD modeling, assembly design, and product visualization using Fusion 360. It improved understanding of parametric modeling techniques, mechanical component design, and engineering drafting practices.

The process also enhanced problem-solving skills, design thinking, and the ability to convert engineering ideas into accurate 3D models and assemblies.
